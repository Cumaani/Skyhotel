About Us

Sky Hotel opened its doors January 2015.

Starting as a small one storey hall, the idea was to always aim for a top class hotel with fully operational restaurants. In 2019, that finally came to realization as Sky Hotel once again opened its doors completely renovated and upgraded.Keeping the welcoming lobby but making additions to the once small one storey hall and restaurant, converted Sky Hotel into what is now a towering 7 storey hotel in the heart of prestigious Masalaha neighbourhood in Hargeisa.
